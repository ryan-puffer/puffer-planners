Hi, I'm Stormye!

When I was in college, I fell in love with having a planner that was practical AND beautiful. When I became a teacher, I bought a planner from every major planner publisher in a search for the PERFECT planner. While they were all wonderful, each had something that I would change!

So, in 2018, I decided to create my own!

It's been a long road, but the result of all this hard work is a planner that I am truly proud of. I hope you enjoy using it as much as I enjoyed making it!
